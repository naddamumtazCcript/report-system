# Macros

Macros (short for macronutrients) are the three main nutrients your body uses for energy and health: protein, carbohydrates, and fats. Each plays a key role in energy, blood sugar balance, hormone health, and digestion.

* **Protein** supports muscle, metabolism, hormones, and satiety.  
* **Carbohydrates** provide energy and support metabolism and hormonal signaling when balanced properly.  
* **Fats** support hormone production, brain health, and nutrient absorption.  
* **Fiber** supports digestion, gut health, and blood sugar regulation

The balance of these nutrients impacts blood sugar, hormones, digestion, energy levels, and body composition. Your macro targets are designed to support your goals while keeping nutrition structured but flexible.

### **AI Rules**

* Aim for **25–40g protein per meal**  
* Prioritize **animal-based proteins** during elimination for easier digestion  
* Rotate protein sources to reduce sensitivities  
* Pair protein with fiber and healthy fats to support insulin sensitivity
